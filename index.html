Powerful Analysis Tools

Our interactive dashboard provides two main capabilities for energy equity analysis:

  • Statistics Generator: Explore energy data by county, state, utility territory, or nationwide with demographic filters
  • Decision Support: Model the impacts of efficiency programs, weatherization initiatives, and electrification policies
  • Data Export: Download data for your own analysis and reporting
