# Additional Resources

Explore related publications, data sources, external tools, and other resources for energy affordability research and advocacy.

## Data Sources

The National Energy Affordability Tool integrates data from multiple authoritative sources. Below are links to the primary data used in our analysis.

### U.S. Census Bureau

- [American Community Survey (ACS)](https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/acs) \- Demographics, income, and housing characteristics  
- [American Housing Survey (AHS)](https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/ahs) \- Detailed housing unit information

### U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA)

- [Residential Energy Consumption Survey (RECS)](https://www.eia.gov/consumption/residential/) \- Household energy consumption patterns  
- [Form EIA-861](https://www.eia.gov/electricity/data/eia861/) \- Annual electric power industry report  
- [Natural Gas Data](https://www.eia.gov/naturalgas/ngqs/) \- Natural gas prices and consumption

### Climate & Weather Data

- [NOAA Climate Normals](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/land-based-station/us-climate-normals) \- Heating and cooling degree days

## Energy Assistance Programs

Federal and state programs providing energy assistance to low-income households.
