Data Sources

The National Energy Affordability Tool integrates data from multiple authoritative sources. Below are links to the primary data used in our analysis.

U.S. Census Bureau

  • American Community Survey (ACS) - Demographics, income, and housing characteristics
  • American Housing Survey (AHS) - Detailed housing unit information

U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA)

  • Residential Energy Consumption Survey (RECS) - Household energy consumption patterns
  • Form EIA-861 - Annual electric power industry report
  • Natural Gas Data - Natural gas prices and consumption

Climate & Weather Data

  • NOAA Climate Normals - Heating and cooling degree days

About PSE Healthy Energy

PSE Healthy Energy is a non-profit energy science and policy research institute dedicated to supplying evidence-based, scientific information on the public health, environmental, and climate dimensions of energy production and use.
